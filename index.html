REDDOT

A novel twist on social media apps that relies on community consensus to attach captions to pictures. It has a voting/coment system not unlike Reddit (hence the name). A full-stack app built on React.js, Ruby on Rails, and PostgreSQL.

SHIP-IT

A prototype for a peer-to-peer goods delivery app, which integrates Google Maps / Places / Routes APIs on the front-end. This was a collaboration between 3 developers, and our first experience with Ruby on Rails. The front-end is powered on Javascript and React.js.


BEER LOBANG

Record your favorite beer locations with an app that integrates Google maps with custom markers for your most beloved bars! Relies on Node.js / PostgreSQL on the back-end; uses React components and Google APIs for the front-end.
